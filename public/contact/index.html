SalmonVision processes video from underwater cameras, weirs, fish ladders, drone footage, and other monitoring setups. Our models are trained on diverse conditions including varying water clarity, lighting, and camera angles. We support most standard video formats and can help optimize your camera setup for best results.

Our models achieve >95% accuracy for salmon detection and counting in optimal conditions, with species classification accuracy of 90-95% depending on video quality and species present. Performance varies based on water clarity, lighting, and camera positioning. We provide confidence scores with all detections and support human-in-the-loop validation workflows.
